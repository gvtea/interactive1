    2011-now

    Forums or chatrooms aren't as popular as they were in the early to mid 2000's. Instagram and other social media websites have replaced this clunky need for communication. Posts can be uploaded instantly and people chat using the comments section or through direct/private messaging.

    Procreate isn't the only digital art software around. Some others that I've used include ChibiPaint, Fire Alpaca, and Paint Tool SAI-all of which are free to download or pirate.
