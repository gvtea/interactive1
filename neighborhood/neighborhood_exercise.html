    Sign Hill Park

    The park is known for its hillside letters that spell out
    "South San Francisco The Industrial City"

    With many hiking trails that loop around with a view of Colma, Daly City, the airport, and the San Bruno Mountains, it is breathtaking to visit.

    Spruce Cafe


    Tucked away at a slight intersection on El Camino Real is this small, contemporary cafe nestled at a corner of a residential street, Spruce.
    They sell a variety of sweet and savory pastries and, of course, lattes of many flavors.

    Bunn Mi Cafe
